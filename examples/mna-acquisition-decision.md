# Example — Should we acquire a smaller competitor?

## Input

> We are a 50-person SaaS company with RMB 20M ARR. We have an opportunity to acquire a 20-person competitor team for RMB 30M. Should we do it?

## What a strong answer should do

A high-quality answer should:

1. classify this as a **Type 1 / one-way-door decision**
2. clarify core constraints: cash, integration bandwidth, strategic fit, speed, retention risk
3. present at least 4 options:
   - acquire now
   - do not acquire
   - acqui-hire / partial asset deal
   - commercial partnership first
4. include explicit **do-nothing consequences**
5. assess:
   - valuation reasonableness
   - synergy assumptions
   - cultural integration risk
   - customer overlap / churn risk
   - talent retention risk
6. give a recommendation with:
   - confidence level
   - what would change the recommendation
   - next steps and owners

## Useful analysis angles

- Revenue multiple vs growth profile
- Cross-sell upside vs integration drag
- Best / base / worst case outcomes
- Whether this is truly a market consolidation move or just expensive panic-buying
- What a competitor or investor would infer from the deal

## Example output skeleton

```markdown
## Decision Brief: Competitor Acquisition

**Urgency:** 🟡 This Week
**Confidence Level:** Medium

### The Question
Should we spend RMB 30M to acquire a smaller competitor team now?

### Context & Constraints
- ARR: RMB 20M
- Team size: 50
- Target team size: 20
- Asking price: RMB 30M
- Constraint: integration capacity is limited

### Options Analysis
#### Option A — Acquire now
...
#### Option B — Do not acquire
...
#### Option C — Acqui-hire / asset-only structure
...
#### Option D — Partnership first
...

### Recommendation
I recommend Option C because ...

### What would change my mind
- If retention terms are secured for the top 5 critical people
- If synergy evidence is stronger than assumed

### Next Steps
1. Build a 90-day integration plan
2. Validate customer overlap and churn risk
3. Model downside cash impact
```
